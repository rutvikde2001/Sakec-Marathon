FAQ's:

Where can we run?

You can choose to run anywhere or any route you prefer, as per the mentioned guidelines.

Where can we get the t-shirts and medals?

Within 30 days post the event via courier.

How to get E-Certificates?

You will be provided the E-Certificates from the Google link or to your email around 5 days after the event.

Which application can be used for running?

You will have to use our SAKEC Marathon App only for running.
